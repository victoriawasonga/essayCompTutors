Rivision policy

What Happens If You Need Your Paper Revised?

 While we provide high-quality academic assistance,essay revisions are sometimes inevitable.
  If it happens that you need a revision from us,we are always ready to revise your paper
  for free as long as it is in line with our main terms which are:

  1. Revision instructions should always remain the same and should not conflict with initial instructions
  2. Revisions can be requested at any time before approving order but you have a 7 day grace period in which to request for a revision.
  3. Deadline date for revision should be close to your initial deadline date
  4. Youre required to contact our customer support team and provide further instructions regarding to the changes you are requesting for.

 Please Note:

  You have two weeks to “Approve” the revised paper starting from the moment we upload the last version to your personal account.
  After which, if there’s no “Approval” or further revision request actions performed, then the order is automatically
 approved by our system. This is done to facilitate early payments to all our writers.

 If you require significant changes to your original order or request a revision with us 7 days after your order is approved,
  we will request you to pay an extra fee. Please contact our customer service staff for an estimate.

 In case you request for a different writer, remember that additional time is needed to find a new writer.
 Thus, you’ll need to adjust your revision deadline accordingly. We propose adding an extra 12 hours to the deadline for the new writer.

 Please note that we reserve the rights to extend any revision deadline as per our writers’ request in some cases.
  However, this extension will only be done if the timeframe you have initially paid for allows such.
